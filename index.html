About Avarniya

The students of ARISE form a colloquium of talented students and organize the most anticipated fest of the year.It is conducted exclusively by students belonging to the branch of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science. As part of this engaging two-day festival, students have the opportunity to participate in technical, non-technical events, workshops, and win exciting cash prizes. Keeping with its theme of living innovation, AVARNIYA is ready for its first iteration.
